I am fascinated by the possibility of robots coexisting with humans at comparable intelligence levels and it is a dream that I constantly strive towards. Accordingly, my interests in robotics lie in the field of Human-Robot Interaction (HRI) and Motion Planning.

Currently, I am a PhD Student in the Interactive and Collaborative Autonomous Robotics Lab at the University of Southern California (USC), advised by Dr. Stefanos Nikolaidis. My research focuses on developing intelligent robots that can understand and predict human behaviour to collaborate with humans under uncertainty.

Apart from my interests in human-robot interaction (HRI), I am also fascinated by space exploration and interstellar travel. One of my life goals is to be able to apply the concepts of robotics like estimation, planning, localisation, mapping, and reinforcement learning for planetary exploration and even HRI in space operations.
