Music

I compose music inspired from my life experiences.

Charmander, named after the Pokemon, is a lofi guitar beat made for playing in the background while studying or sitting by the fire. Wildfire, my best song thus far, is a sattire on the wildfires that blazed amidst the madness of the 2020 pandemic.
