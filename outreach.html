K-12 Outreach

Spring 2023: I conducted lab sessions with Bryon Tjanaka at the Synergy Quantum Academy High School on assembly and programming of VEC Medbots. I also facilitated the participation of students and parents in a human-robot collaboration demo on family science night.


Summer 2021, 2022: I mentored high-school students as part of the Summer High School Intensive in Next-Generation Engineering (SHINE) program at USC.
